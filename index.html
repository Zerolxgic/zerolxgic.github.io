About Me

Hello! I'm Zerrius, a self-taught developer and gaming content creator who loves building things, whether it's inside a game engine or out in the real world.

I'm driven by creativity, curiosity, and the challenge of learning new skills from the ground up. I have ADHD, so I build systems that work with my brain: knowledge vaults, modular workflows, and AI collaboration tools that help me stay organized, keep context, and learn faster.

I enjoy designing virtual worlds, improving systems, and crafting experiences that feel smooth, fun, and intentional. I learn by experimenting, and I'm not afraid to rebuild something if it leads to a cleaner result.

I've used AI in my workflow for years, treating it like a power tool: it helps me move faster and solve problems more efficiently, but I'm always the one driving. The goal is understanding, not shortcuts.

When I'm not working on a project, I'm spending time with my family, learning something new, or relaxing with a game. This website shares my journey and the projects I've built so far, with more updates as I keep leveling up.
